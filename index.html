The Crew

JN Creative is a one-person studio built around two crafts that usually live in separate worlds. On one side, building apps and websites — writing the logic, shaping the interface, shipping the product. On the other, picking up a camera — framing a shot, cutting a sequence, timing a story to music.

Most people specialize in one lane. JN Creative exists because the two feed each other: a dev background means every video project gets edited with the same attention to structure and flow as a piece of software, and a shooter's eye means every app and site gets built with a real sense of composition, not just function.

Whether the deliverable is a working app, a finished website, or a final export ready to publish, the same process applies — plan it properly, build it carefully, and polish it until it's ready to ship.
